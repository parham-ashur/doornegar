How it works:

  1. Ingestion: RSS feeds from news sources are fetched every 15 minutes → articles stored in PostgreSQL
  2. NLP Processing: Articles go through keyword extraction and TF-IDF embedding generation
  3. Clustering: Articles with similar embeddings (cosine similarity > 0.7) are grouped into "stories"
  4. Bias Scoring: Each article in a story is sent to Claude AI for political alignment, framing, tone, and factuality analysis
  5. Frontend: The Next.js website fetches stories from the API and displays them with comparison views, bias spectrums, and coverage bars
